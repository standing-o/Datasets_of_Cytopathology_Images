# Datasets of Cytopathology Images

- A collection of dataset consists of 20 publicly available (slide/patch) images for cytopathology.
- These datasets can help you create robust machine/deep learning models for the patch-level diagnosis or slide-level diagnosis.

## **Publicly Available Dataset**
### [CCAgT](https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/wg4bpm33hj/2)

> Atkinson Amorim, João Gustavo; Matias, André; Bottamedi, Tainee; Sanches, Vinícius; Costa, Ane Francyne; Onofre, Fabiana; Onofre, Alexandre; Wangenheim, Aldo (2022), “CCAgT: Images of Cervical Cells with AgNOR Stain Technique”, Mendeley Data, V2, doi: 10.17632/wg4bpm33hj.2

- **Cervical Cancer**
- 9339 images, Resolution of 1600×1200 where each pixel is 0.111µmX0.111µm.
- Stained with AgNOR.


### [Cervix93](https://github.com/parham-ap/cytology_dataset/tree/master)

> Phoulady, Hady Ahmady, and Peter R. Mouton. "A new cervical cytology dataset for nucleus detection and image classification (Cervix93) and methods for cervical nucleus detection." arXiv preprint arXiv:1811.09651 (2018).

- **Cervical Cancer**
- 93 image stacks and EDF, 40x magnification, ThinPrep Pap-stained.


### [Mendeley-LBC](https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/zddtpgzv63/4)

> Hussain, Elima (2019), “Liquid based cytology pap smear images for multi-class diagnosis of cervical cancer”, Mendeley Data, V4, doi: 10.17632/zddtpgzv63.4

- **Cervical Cancer**
- 40x magnification using Leica ICC50 HD microscope, Pap smear, LBC.


### [APC](https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/ytd568rh3p/1)
> Cuña Cabrera, Pablo; Pachiarotti, Virginia Eva; Guerra, Rodrigo (2024), “APCData cervical cytology cells”, Mendeley Data, V1, doi: 10.17632/ytd568rh3p.1

- **Cervical Cancer**
- 425 high-quality microscope field images, with 2048 x 1532 pixel size.
- Olympus CX40RF100 microscope, with a 40x objective lens, a 10x eyepiece, and an Olympus LC30 Optical Microscope camera.


### [Body Cavity Fluid](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/cmacus/body-cavity-fluid-cytology-images)
> Sanyal, Parikshit, et al. "A machine learning model for screening of body fluid cytology smears." bioRxiv (2021): 2021-07.

- **Body Fluid Cytology Smears**
- The smears are Leishman stained, photographed at 40X magnification, then resized to 256 x 192 pixel.


### [Bone Marrow Cell](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/andrewmvd/bone-marrow-cell-classification)
> Matek, C., Krappe, S., Münzenmayer, C., Haferlach, T., & Marr, C. (2021). An Expert-Annotated Dataset of Bone Marrow Cytology in Hematologic Malignancies [Data set]. The Cancer Imaging Archive. https://doi.org/10.7937/TCIA.AXH3-T579

- **Bone Marrow Biopsy**
- 170,000+ de-identified, expert-annotated cells from the bone marrow smears of 945 patients.
- Stained by May-Grünwald-Giemsa/Pappenheim, Brightfield microscope with 40x magnification and oil immersion.


### [AML-Cytomorphology_LMU](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/livepriyanka09/aml-cytology/data)
- Single cell Leukocytes from AML Patients and Non-malignant Controls


### [TF Cytology](https://zenodo.org/records/10813218)
> Szczęsna-Iskander, D. (2024). TF Cytology [Data set]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.10813218


### [AICCS](https://zenodo.org/records/10828395)
> Jue Wang. (2024). Artificial Intelligence Enables Precision Diagnosis of Cervical Cytology Grades and Cervical Cancer (1.0.0) [Data set]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.10828395

- **Cervical Cancer**


### [Breast Cyto](https://zenodo.org/records/7890874)
> Berezsky, O., Datsko, T., & Melnyk, G. (2023). Cytological and histological images of breast cancer [Data set]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.7890874

- 3264 x 2448 pixel size, microscope with 40x lens.


### [Cell Instance](https://zenodo.org/records/5938893)
> Alexandre Bouyssoux, Riadh Fezzani, & Jean-Christophe Olivo-Marin. (2022). Cell Instance Segmentation Dataset (1.0.0) [Data set]. IEEE International Symposium on Biomedical Imaging (ISBI), ITC Royal Bengal, Kolkata, India. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.5938893

- 30 digital cytology slides stained with nine variations of Papanicolaou staining. 
- The cytology slides are prepared from urine samples from healthy patients, using a Hologic ThinPrep®5000 processor, and routinely stained with the Agilent Dako CoverStainer®. 
- The slides are finally digitized using a Hamamatsu NanoZoomer®S360 with 21 focal planes and centered on the best focus plane determined by the scanner autofocus.


### [Urine Cyto](https://dataverse.harvard.edu/dataset.xhtml?persistentId=doi:10.7910/DVN/JHZCCM)
> Siddig, emmanuel, 2019, "Replication Data for: Urine cytology in our daily practice", https://doi.org/10.7910/DVN/JHZCCM, Harvard Dataverse, V1, UNF:6:QI5RxH19bMjvAVYeI5Pg2g== [fileUNF]

- Voided urine sample in cytological examination.


### [ISBI 2014](https://cs.adelaide.edu.au/~carneiro/isbi14_challenge/dataset.html)
> “Zhi Lu, Gustavo Carneiro, Andrew P. Bradley, Daniela Ushizima, Masoud S. Nosrati, Andrea G. C. Bianchi, Claudia M. Carneiro, and Ghassan Hamarneh. Evaluation of Three Algorithms for the Segmentation of Overlapping Cervical Cells. IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (J-BHI). Jan 2015 (Accepted).”

- **Cervical Cancer**
- 16 EDF cell images and 945 synthetic images.


### [SIPaKMeD](https://www.cs.uoi.gr/~marina/sipakmed.html)
> Marina E. Plissiti, Panagiotis Dimitrakopoulos, Giorgos Sfikas, Christophoros Nikou, Olga Krikoni, Antonia Charchanti, SIPAKMED: A new dataset for feature and image based classification of normal and pathological cervical cells in Pap smear images, IEEE International Conference on Image Processing (ICIP) 2018, Athens, Greece, 7-10 October 2018. 

- **Cervical Cancer**
- 4,049 cervical cells in 5 categories.
- Cropped from 966 cluster cell images of Pap smear slides.


### [Mendeley-BTTFA](https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/jks43dkjj7/1)
> Zhang, Jianwei (2019), “Data for: Binary Tree-like Network with Two-path Fusion Attention Feature for Cervical Cell Nucleus Segmentation ”, Mendeley Data, V1, doi: 10.17632/jks43dkjj7.1

- **Cervical Cancer**
- 104 cervical LBC images.


### [Cric](https://database.cric.com.br/downloads)
> Cric searchable image database as a public platform for conventional pap smear cytology data - Mariana T. Rezende, Raniere Silva, Fagner de O. Bernardo, Alessandra H. G. Tobias, Paulo H. C. Oliveira, Tales M. Machado, Caio S. Costa, Fatima N. S. Medeiros, Daniela M. Ushizima, Claudia M. Carneiro and Andrea G. C. Bianchi, published in the Scientific Data - Nature in June 2021.

- **Cervical Cancer**
- 400 images with 11,534 cells.
- Microscope slides of the uterine cervix using the conventional smear (Pap smear).


### [CCEDD](https://github.com/nachifur/LLPC)
> Liu, Jiawei, et al. "Local label point correction for edge detection of overlapping cervical cells." Frontiers in Neuroinformatics 16 (2022): 895290.

- **Cervical Cancer**
- 686 raw cervical images and 33,614 cut images.


### [Cx22](https://github.com/LGQ330/Cx22)
> Liu, Guangqi, et al. "Cx22: A new publicly available dataset for deep learning-based segmentation of cervical cytology images." Computers in Biology and Medicine 150 (2022): 106194.

- **Cervical Cancer**
- 1,320 images of 14,946 cellular instances (cytoplasm and nucleus).


### [UFSC OCPap](https://arquivos.ufsc.br/d/5035aec3c24f421a95d0/)
> Matias, André Victória, et al. "Segmentation, detection, and classification of cell nuclei on oral cytology samples stained with papanicolaou." SN Computer Science 2.4 (2021): 285.

- 1934 images of 1200 × 1600 pixels acquired from two slides of cancer diagnosed oral brush samples stained with Papanicolaou.
- Captured with an Axio Scan.Z1 microscope and a Hitachi HV-F202SCL camera.


### [BMCD-FGCD](https://github.com/JustlfC03/SCKansformer)
> Chen, Yifei, et al. "Sckansformer: Fine-grained classification of bone marrow cells via kansformer backbone and hierarchical attention mechanisms." IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (2024).

- Bone Marrow Blood Cell Fine-Grained Classification
